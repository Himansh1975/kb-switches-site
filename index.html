About Us

Learn more about B.R ENTERPRISE and our journey in home automation

B.R ENTERPRISE

Established in 2019, B.R ENTERPRISE has been a pioneer in the home automation industry under the registered brands KB Switches and KB Lights. Started under the guidance of our company MD, we have been on a journey to expand across India with innovative smart home solutions.

Our business focuses on Home Automation systems (both semi and fully automatic), Smart Switches, and Lighting Products including T5, bulbs, sensor lights, and more. We aim to solve real problems using smart technology throughout India.

As an MSME Certified Manufacturing Company in India, we take pride in our "Made in India" products that meet high standards of quality and comply with all regulations and safety norms. Our team uses the latest testing methods to ensure quality before delivery.
